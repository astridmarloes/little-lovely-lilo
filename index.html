Hi there bunny lover! Welcome to our website. We are Whisky, Lilo and Ollie and we are the sweetest buns in the whole wide world (most of the time). Come and join us on our adventures! Our hobbies are eating, sleeping and annoying our human!

Whisky ♂

  • Breed: Mini Rex
  • Colour: Cinnamon
  • DOB: 18 March 2016

Whisky is the man of the house and he is a our sweet little cinnamon roll. He is a good boy and a very friendly bun, but he is sensitive and is easily scared. Whisky loves treats and will come running at the first signs of food!

Lilo ♀

  • Breed: Mini Lop
  • Colour: White
  • DOB: 11 April 2017

Lilo is definitely the boss of the bunny bunch! She is very possessive and dominant and can even be a little moody sometimes, but she loves Whisky and she takes good care of him. She is very curious and loves a good head rub!

Ollie ♀

  • Breed: Dwarf Mix
  • Colour: Cinnamon
  • DOB: 21 March 2020

Ollie is the baby of the family! She is a curious, easy going and cuddly bunny. She can be very hyperactive but also loves to fall asleep in the most random places. She has already had to deal with a lot of health problems but she is a little fighter and has stayed strong and positive through it all!
